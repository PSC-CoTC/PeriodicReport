## Use the following settings:
## 
## Parameter: annual temperature, 
## Time Scale: 12-Month, 
## Month: September
## Start Year: 1895
## End Year: [current year]
## Region: Northwest
## 
## Download as CSV, save as "NOAA-temperature.csv" into the data folder
